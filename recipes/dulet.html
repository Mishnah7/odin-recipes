Description

Dulet is a traditional Ethiopian dish made from minced lamb or beef, mixed with spices and served with injera. It is a flavorful dish often enjoyed as a starter or main course.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound minced lamb or beef
  • 1/4 cup niter kibbeh (clarified butter)
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chili peppers, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon mitmita (Ethiopian spice blend)
  • Salt to taste
  • Injera for serving

Steps:

  1. Cook the Onions:
    • In a pan, heat niter kibbeh and saut√© the chopped onions until translucent.
  2. Add Meat:
    • Add the minced lamb or beef to the pan and cook until browned.
  3. Add Spices:
    • Add mitmita, green chili peppers, and salt. Mix well and cook for an additional 5-10 minutes.
  4. Serve:
    • Serve hot with injera on the side.

Notes:

  • Dulet can be made with various meats, including beef or goat.
  • Adjust the spice level according to your preference.
